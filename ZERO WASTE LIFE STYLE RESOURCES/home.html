Introduction

The Zero Waste lifestyle encourages reducing waste, making sustainable choices, and preserving the environment through intentional living and smart consumption.

Core Principles

  1. Refuse what you don’t need
  2. Reduce what you do need
  3. Reuse whatever you can
  4. Recycle what you can’t reuse
  5. Rot (compost) the rest

Daily Tips

  • Carry reusable shopping bags
  • Use stainless steel bottles and lunch boxes
  • Compost your organic waste
  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging

FAQs

Is Zero Waste realistic?

It's a journey. Any step toward less waste makes a big difference.

Can I compost at home?

Yes! Even apartment dwellers can compost with small indoor bins or bokashi systems.
