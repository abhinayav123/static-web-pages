🌟 What is Biohacking?

Biohacking is the art and science of optimizing your body and mind using self-experimentation, data, and technology. From nootropics to intermittent fasting, it's all about enhancing performance.

🍏 Nutrition

Biohacking starts with what you eat. Key elements include:

  • Low-carb high-fat diets like Keto
  • Anti-inflammatory foods
  • Microbiome-friendly meals
  • Adaptogens (Ashwagandha, Rhodiola)
